# Problem Statement

## Current Industry Challenge

Large enterprises generate and manage massive amounts of unstructured video content including:

* Marketing advertisements
* Financial product promotions
* Insurance policy videos
* Healthcare awareness campaigns
* Customer onboarding videos
* Employee training sessions
* Social media promotional content

These videos must comply with strict organizational, legal, and regulatory standards.

---

# Existing Problems

## 1. Manual Compliance Auditing

Current compliance review processes are heavily manual.

Teams must:

* Watch videos manually
* Verify disclaimers
* Review spoken statements
* Validate on-screen text
* Cross-check regulatory policies

This process is:

* Slow
* Expensive
* Error-prone
* Non-scalable

---

## 2. Regulatory Risk

Failure to identify compliance violations can result in:

* Legal penalties
* Financial fines
* Brand reputation damage
* Customer trust loss
* Regulatory escalations

---

## 3. Lack of Explainability

Traditional moderation systems often fail to provide:

* Timestamp evidence
* Root cause explanations
* Traceable audit decisions
* Regulatory mapping

---

## 4. Operational Bottlenecks

Compliance teams struggle with:

* High review volumes
* Delayed approvals
* Inconsistent human decisions
* Lack of centralized governance

---

# Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-powered multimodal compliance intelligence platform that:

* Automatically ingests video content
* Extracts transcripts and OCR text
* Retrieves relevant compliance policies using RAG
* Uses LLM reasoning for violation detection
* Generates structured compliance reports
* Provides enterprise-grade observability and explainability

---

# Business Outcome

The system will:

* Reduce manual auditing efforts
* Improve compliance accuracy
* Accelerate approval cycles
* Minimize legal risks
* Enable scalable AI governance
* Improve operational efficiency
